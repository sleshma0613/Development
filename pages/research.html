MY RESEARCH

To construct this website themed vintage watches, we have taken references form a variety of inspiration are mentioned below:

The Insight website features a neat and professional design, with well-defined content areas. It adopts a corporate style that strikes a good balance between images and text, making it easy to read. The aim is to deliver information in a tidy and structures way.

The design of About Us page of this website draws from a well-structured content layout while blending in a vintage aesthetic. It points out the aim of sharing the brands story in straightforward and engaging way.

Netflix login page features a sleek dark background filled with a grid of movie posters. The login form is in the centre of the page which is designed to be straightforward and user-friendly. It emphasizes a captivating user experience, showcasing vibrant red color and striking contrast with black.

The login page design takes reference from Netflix, our goal was to create a sleek and user-friendly interface for quick access. For our website which is vintage themed, the login page showcases clock background. It gives a brighter, classic look that complements the vintage style.

The Titan website is a well organized grid that presents watches sorted by brand, emphasizing a contemporary and insights for user-friendly online shopping experience.

The design for this page is takes reference from Titans website, which effectively highlights watches in a clear layout. This design maintains a comparable grid structure while adding touch of vintage charm.

The website of Rolex showcases a design centered around luxury, boasting a refined and stylish homepage. It uses stunning visuals and short texts to emphasize a sense of exclusivity. Its website presents a classic aesthetic, admirably featuring a detailed close-up of their watches.

The homepage of NovaTimes adopts a vintage and aesthetic appearance, perfectly complementing the concept of the website. It features a neutral, gold and toned background tht highlights its vintage elegance. This creates a sense of timeless and classic beauty, also captures the life of vintage watches.

The ABlogToWatch website features a modern blog layout that showcases recent articles in a grid. It emphasizes news, reviews and trends about watches, offering brief previews for each piece. The layout of this website is organized for easy browsing, enabling users to see the latest updates easily.

NovaTimes adopts a stylish, magazine-style layout. It holds a classic, vintage theme with warm color palate and a revolutionary design that aligns with the brands identity. The content combines storytelling with informative pieces, highlighting history and art of collection of vintage watches.
