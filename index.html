ABOUT TECHNOLOGY

Technology has taken over every aspect of our lives, transforming communication, shopping, and even the appreciation of art. In recent years, there has been a significant change in the watch sector. Digital platforms have made it possible for collectors to display their works and interact with other collectors across the world in virtual marketplaces. We can now inspect watches in previously unheard details thanks to high-resolution image and video technology. More precise identification of old watches is now possible thanks to advanced testing technology.
